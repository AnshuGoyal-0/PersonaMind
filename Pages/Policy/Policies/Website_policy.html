  • The remaining balance is due upon completion and approval of the final deliverables.

2.4. Ownership of Work

2.6. Confidentiality

  • All client information and project details are kept confidential.

2.7. Dispute Resolution

  • Disputes will first be resolved through informal negotiation.

2.8. Liability
